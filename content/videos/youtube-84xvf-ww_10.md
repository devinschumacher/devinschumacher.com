OVERVIEW –\n\n# AI Voice Cloning & TTS Settings\n\n### Voice Exaggeration\n- **What it does:** Controls how expressive and emotional the voice sounds.  \n- **Low:** Flat, neutral, robotic-like delivery.  \n- **High:** Dramatic, lively, almost theatrical delivery.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Low  corporate training videos, calm narrations.  \n- High  storytelling, podcasts, dramatic reads.  \n\n## \U0001F3B2 Randomness & Creativity\n\n### Temperature\n- **What it does:** Controls how much variety the AI puts into speech.  \n- **Low:** Predictable, steady delivery.  \n- **High:** More surprises — varied pacing, tone, and phrasing.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Low  serious or professional voiceovers.  \n- High  fun, natural-sounding conversations.  \n\n### Top P\n- **What it does:** Controls how adventurous the AI is when choosing *how to say* something.  \n- **Low (0.1–0.3):** Very safe, plain delivery — may sound robotic but consistent.  \n- **High (0.8–1.0):** More natural variation, pauses, and human-like rhythm.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Low  strict narration, tutorials, or formal content.  \n- High  relaxed conversation or casual storytelling.  \n\n### Min P\n- **What it does:** Filters out unlikely word choices or odd intonations.  \n- **Low:** AI might say quirky or weird-sounding things.  \n- **High:** AI avoids oddities and sticks to safer, more natural phrasing.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Raise if the voice sounds \"off\" or unnatural.  \n- Lower if you want more casual, imperfect \"human-like\" vibes.  \n\n## ⚖️ Script Adherence\n\n### Flow CFG Scale\n- **What it does:** Controls how tightly the voice sticks to your **reference voice** and the exact wording.  \n- **Low:** Looser and more natural; may drift a bit from the sample's tone or pacing.  \n- **High:** Closer match to the sample's tone and timing; reads more exactly, but can sound a bit stiff if cranked too high.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Low  podcasts, dialogue, casual speaking styles.  \n- High  tight voice match, ads/brand lines, ADR, e-learning where consistency matters.  \n\n### CFG Weight\n- **What it does:** Adjusts how strong that \"follow the text\" rule is.  \n- **Low:** The AI might slip in filler or natural re-phrasing.  \n- **High:** Reads word-for-word, like a teleprompter.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Medium  best balance (natural but accurate).  \n- High  official messages, legal scripts, or precise readings.  \n\n## \U0001F501 Repetition & Length\n\n### Repetition Penalty\n- **What it does:** Stops the AI from repeating itself.  \n- **Low:** Might loop words or phrases.  \n- **High:** Forces variety (but too high can make speech unnatural).  \n- **Use it for:** Raise slightly if you notice repetition.  \n\n### Max New Tokens\n- **What it does:** Limits how long the voice can speak.  \n- **Think of it as:** A maximum word count.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Short clips  set low.  \n- Long narrations or speeches  set high.  \n\n## \U0001F9EA Stability & Consistency\n\n### Seed (Optional)\n- **What it does:** Controls whether each generation sounds the same or changes.  \n- **Random (default):** Each take sounds a little different.  \n- **Fixed number:** Exact same take every time.  \n- **Use it for:**  \n- Random  experimenting, creative voices.  \n- Fixed  professional projects where consistency matters.  \n\n---\n\n## ✅ Quick Preset Ideas\n\n**Professional Narration (safe & clear):**  \n- Voice Exaggeration: Low  \n- Temperature: Low  \n- Top P: Low  \n- Flow CFG: High  \n- CFG Weight: High  \n\n**Podcast / Storytelling (engaging & natural):**  \n- Voice Exaggeration: Medium  \n- Temperature: Medium  \n- Top P: High  \n- Flow CFG: Medium  \n- CFG Weight: Medium  \n\n**Fun / Creative Mode (casual & lively):**  \n- Voice Exaggeration: High  \n- Temperature: High  \n- Top P: High  \n- Flow CFG: Low  \n- CFG Weight: Low  \n\n---\n\n## Final Thoughts\n\nEach of these settings is a tool to shape the voice.  \n- Want **clear and exact**?  keep things low and controlled.  \n- Want **natural and human-like**?  raise variety and expressiveness.  \n- Want **repeatable results**?  lock in a seed.  \n\nStart with the presets above, then **tweak one knob at a time** while listening to the output. That's the fastest way to find your perfect setup.\n\n– \U0001F3F7️ TAGS –"
